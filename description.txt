This mod provides tile palette, allowing easy access to place many kinds of tiles with or without a paint on them.
How to use:
1. Set a hotkey to toggle visibility of the palette.
2. Click white-based red cross icon ("Add preset" button) to register a selected tile in your inventory. A paint at bottom rightmost in your inventory will be applied while Paint Sprayer is on.
3. Click colored icon to select a registered tile preset in your inventory. Note that the color on the palette is one shown in the map menu.
4. (Additional) Click "Delete" button on the bottom right to delete a selected tile preset. You can also sort tile presets by dragging and dropping the icons.
